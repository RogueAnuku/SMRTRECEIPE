Smart Receipt Design the Case Study

Conducted between April 12th-April 30th, 2023.

Team: Anika McPhee (UX Researcher), Faye Teodoro (UX Designer), Vaishali Singh (UX Designer), Hill Phatpanichot (UX Designer).

Background:

Our team was challenged to create a mobile app concept that solves a need in users’ everyday lives.

Challenge:

Significant number of people struggle with finding new and exciting recipes that cater to their food preferences.

Resolution:

Our mobile application, Home Chef, is crafted to assist individuals with hectic schedules in utilizing ingredients readily available in their cupboards, thereby saving both time and money. Powered by artificial intelligence, the app customizes nutritious recipes according to users' meal preferences. Additionally, it provides alternative ingredients for added flexibility and for maximized benefit.

The Method Toolbox:

  • Proto-persona
  • Journey Mapping
  • Interviews
  • Online Survey
    • Affinity Diagram
  • Online Survey
  • Empathy Mapping
  • Competitor Analysis
  • Wireframing
  • User Testing
  • High-Fidelity Prototyping

Findings:

66.7% of users experience pain points related to the length of time healthy meal preparation takes and sited that as a reason meal planning does not occur. 33.3% of users struggled with finding healthy meal options for their unique dietary restrictions.

The Target Demographic:

Young professionals, age ranges betweeen 18yrs-35yrs.

Based on user research, we've found that busy schedules make healthy meal planning challenging. Users prefer quick and easy recipes, avoiding time-consuming videos or complex dishes. Our goal is to create a recipe app that is convenient and time-efficient, with success measured by reduced meal preparation time.

Young professionals focused on health need an efficient way to plan diverse and nutritious meals due to their hectic schedules, preventing repetitive and less healthy choices that can adversely affect their overall well-being.

Issues we faced...

How do begin? How deep do we plan on diving into the nutrition/health aspect? How much focus do we put on customization and ingredence based receipe recomendations?

We plan on finding out...

With testing our digital sketch designs on recruited parcticpants and then interperate the data!

Summary:

Our team has recognized a thriving market for meal planning apps, and our mobile application distinguishes itself by seamlessly integrating various crucial features to enhance user experience. With its unique combination of functionalities, our app caters to individuals managing budgets, navigating tight schedules, and seeking a convenient solution for planning and organizing meals.
